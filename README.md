I work across four layers. Each one stacks: **Information Architecture** gives the words and structure. **Data Engineering** gives trustworthy data. **Data Analytics** turns it into decisions. **Operations Automation** turns decisions into action that holds.

My purpose is to empower individuals and businesses with the ability to make data-driven decisions. Whether it's helping someone overcome an everyday challenge or delivering insights to a team, I'm dedicated to guidance that leads to better outcomes.
